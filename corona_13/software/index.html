  • CODE RUSH

    C/C++ Programming

    DESCRIPTION:

    "It is not the place for the frightened lizards but for the Programming Wizards." Get down on the battleground and claim the right of being the “BEST PROGRAMMER”.
    This is an offline event which is organized to test your programming skills and knowledge of C/C++. It provides the perfect platform for all the programmers to showcase their skills.
    This event will have 2 rounds:

    First Round(error removal):
    This round is open to all. Participants will be given 1 erroneous code and they have to correct errors from the programs. Participants who correct the errors first in least time (max 15 mins) will be qualified for the next round or in case of tie, participant who corrects the most of the errors will be qualified. This round will test speed, accuracy and ability to optimize.

    Second Round(ultimate programmer):
    The participant shortlisted from the first round will be provided with 2 problems and they have to code that problem in least time (max 30 mins). The participant who codes the program first will be the winner. In case of tie, a tie breaker problem will be provided and solves it first will be declared winner. Top 5 will be getting certificates.


    RULES:

    1. Register online or on the first day of fest till 12 noon. After that no one will be entertained.
    2. The participation for the first round is free.
    3. Any participant using any unfair means will be permanently disqualified from the event.
    4. Maximum of 2 members will be allowed in a team.
    5. No answers will be considered after the allotted time of respective rounds.
