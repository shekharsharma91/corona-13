TASTE BUD

Description:

Taste the bud is the food tasting event. There will be three rounds. Every round is an elimination round. Each round would have 4-5 dishes according to the difficulty level of the round. Participants would be blindfolded, and they would have to taste and identify the dish. If they identify the prescribed minimum number of dishes correctly, they would be qualified for the next round.


Rules:

The piece of cloth would be provided by the organizer only.
Participants are not allowed to bring any material with them. found cheating, then participant would be declared disqualified. And organizers decision will be final regarding this.


Instruction For Players:

‘Taste the bud’ is an individual game. No group or couple entry.
It’s a FIRST COME FIRST PLAY event.
Food and all other things will be provided by the organizers.
All the food provided will be hygienic and fresh. Only vegetarian food will be given in the event.
Prizes would be given to participants who will make it to the final round of the event.
