Creative Writing Tadka Maar ke

DESCRIPTION:

Participants are supposed to fictionalise their own thoughts on different topics that will be given to them and express themselves on a sheet of paper.

RULES:

1. Topics will be provided on the spot.
2. Round 1 would be a pre -eliminary round in which topic will be given on the spot.
3. 45 minutes would be given to each participant.
4. Maximum word limit would be 250 words.
5. 4-5 participants would be promoted to round 2.
6. In round 2 you have to re create a movie scene with new dialogue, characters etc.
7. Decision of judges will be final and binding.
8. Any new changes can be made by the event heads.
